## Bio

- Semilore Idowu is a Fullstack software engineer based out of [Lagos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lagos), [Nigeria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria).
- He is currently in the process of acquiring a Bachelor of Science degree in computer engineering from the [University of Lagos](https://unilag.edu.ng/).
- He has a very sound software engineering background. He can usually be found building Fullstack modern websites using popular [JavaScript](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Learn/JavaScript/First_steps/What_is_JavaScript) frameworks like [React](https://reactjs.org/) and [Node](https://nodejs.org/en/).
- He is passionate about tech in general and hopes to one day solve a problem facing [Nigeria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nigeria) and [Africa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa), in general, using software technologies.
- To this end, he is looking to acquire as much experience as possible by collaborating with the top minds in the software industry.
