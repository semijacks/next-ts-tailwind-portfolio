Bio
---

### Job Title

Lee Robinson, Director of Developer Relations at Vercel

### Long, 3rd Person

Lee Robinson is the Director of Developer Relations at
[Vercel](http://vercel.com/), where he helps developers build a faster
web and leads the Next.js community. Prior to that, Lee was a Senior
Software Engineer focused on the frontend. An educator, writer, and
speaker, Lee has also created extensive courses on React and Next.js.

### Long, 1st Person

Hey, I\'m Lee. I lead Developer Relations at
[Vercel](https://vercel.com/), where my team helps developers build a
faster web. I\'m a Next.js contributor and help lead our open-source
communities. I\'ve passionate about frontend development and have
created courses on React, Next.js, and web development. I\'m an advisor
and investor in early stage startups.

### Short, 3rd Person

Lee Robinson is the Director of Developer Relations at
[Vercel](http://vercel.com/), where he helps developers build a faster
web.

### Short, 1st Person

Hey, I\'m Lee. I lead Developer Relations at
[Vercel](https://vercel.com/), where my team helps developers build a
faster web.

### Education

Lee Robinson graduated from Iowa State University with a BS in Computer
Engineering.
